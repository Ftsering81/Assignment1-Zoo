Marty the Zebra

Marty is a male plains zebra. Plain zebras, also known as common zebras, are the most common of the three species of zebra. They inhabit the grasslands of eastern and southern Africa. Like all zebras, Marty has black and white stripes. Although all plain zebras share similar stripe patterns, no two zebras have exactly the same pattern. Marty is Alex the lion's best friend. He is an energetic and optimisitic zebra.

Plain zebras like Marty are classified as near threatened, with their populations decreasing. The main threats facing plain zebras are human activities such as being hunted for their meat and skin and habitat loss from farming.
