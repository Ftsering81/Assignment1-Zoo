Melman the Giraffe

Melman Mankiewics III is a male reticulated giraffe. Reticulated giraffes, also known as Somali giraffes, are a species of giraffe native to Eastern Africa. Melman is friends with Alex the lion, Marty the zebra, and Gloria the hippo. He is a very nice giraffe and loyal to his friends. He is also a hypochondriac, having doses of medicine for meals and doctor's appointments frequently. Melman is in love with Gloria the hippo.

Reticulated giraffes like Melman are classified as endangered and there are approximately only 9,000 remaining reticulated giraffes. The main threats facing reticulated giraffes are habitat loss and hunting.
